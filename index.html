Our History

Nayi Zindagi Foundation

The 1st fortunate day of the Year 2018 when the foundation brick of Nayi Zindagi Foundation has taken place and subsequently it has come in to existence of registration on the 14 th day of December 2018. It has been mentored by Miss Khadija Khatoon who being compelled by the plenty of valid reasons which led to existence of this unstoppable initiative.(Nayi Zindagi Foundation). The untold life of Miss Khadija in which she experienced scarcity, poverty, impathy and misfortune and these made her more stronger to get rid of the situation. The core question which may comes why the iniaticve of Nayi Zindagi Foundation has been started with Education. The cause of action behind that was the unfortunate sister, Ashima, of Miss Khadija who had been restricted from her school for the little penny of Rs. 40 whereas she was a lady of intelligence, proud and passion, and she wants to be an IPS but she could not go beyond her dream because of acute financial crisis. But her desperate desire of having been educated forced her to attend the school daily and although she was not been allowed to enter into, she used to get her taught through the window of the School. This heart touching incident has given rise to establishment of Nayi Zindagi Foundation for imparting free education to the millions who are needy and greedy for their studies with an objective to make the Education as a weapon of well being of life of Commons.
